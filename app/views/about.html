Our Story

Param Singh

Founder

We believe in creating more inclusive opportunities in food for small-scale food entrepreneurs. Cooking is the most fundamental lever of economic empowerment, and the food industry has taken that away through its strict barriers to entry. We make it easier than ever to build a food business and become your own boss.

We seek to increase access to healthy food options. The current options, like expensive meal-kits and unhealthy fast-food, don't solve the problem. We all love home cooked meals but just don't have the time, skills or passion for creating exciting meals.

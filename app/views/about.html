Our Story



Param Singh

Founder

We believe in creating more inclusive opportunities in food for independent chefs. Cooking is the most fundamental lever of economic empowerment, and the food industry has taken that away through its strict barriers to entry. Chefs can showcase their work, connect with new and existing customers, and build their business.

We seek to increase access to healthy food options. The current options, like expensive meal-kits and unhealthy fast food, don't solve the problem. We all love home cooked meals but just don't have the time, skills or passion for creating exciting meals.

Frequently Asked Questions

Have more questions? Read the full FAQ

How often do I have to cook?

It's your business, so up to you! Our team will work with you to figure out how to best maximize you time and effort. Our most sucessful chefs provide a weeklu menu with order deadlins.

What can I make?

You have ful creative freedom, so make what you love! Most chefs switch up their menu every week, while others go with a few signature dishes. We want you to experiment and own it!

Does it cost anything to be a chef on Orchid Eats?

There's no cost to set up a chef account or post meals. For each order you complete, simply pay the payment processing fee and you keep the rest.

How and when do I get paid?

We use Stripe as our payment processor. Payouts are received 2 days after the order is completed.

Orchid Eats Privacy Policy

Last Revised: 08/25/17

Orchid JSW, Inc. ("Orchid," "us," and "we") is committed to respecting your privacy. We have prepared this Privacy Policy to describe to you our practices regarding personal information we collect from users of our website located at Orchideats.com (the "Site"), the mobile marketplace platform (“Mobile App”) and the Services (as defined below). Orchid provides an online marketplace designed to connects users (each a "Guest") looking for home-cooked meals in their neighborhood with cooks (each a "Chef") who wish to sell meals to Guests (the "Services"). The Mobile App and Site features upcoming available meals (each an "Event"), including information about the Chef and the meal, including pickup information and price.

1. Types of Information We Collect. We collect your information as described below.

(a) Information You Provide to Us.

We may collect personal information from you, such as your name, phone number, e-mail address, zip code and password when you create an account to log in to our network ("Account"). When you post content (text, images, photographs, messages, comments or any other kind of content that is not your e-mail address) on our Mobile App and Site, the information contained in your posting will be stored in our servers and other users of the Mobile App and Site will be able to see it, along with any other information that you choose to make public through your profile ("Profile"). The information that you provide in your Profile will be visible to others, including other users of the Mobile App and Site. We retain information on your behalf, such as files and messages that you store using your Account. We may collect personal information from you when you sign up for Orchid's SMS list or RSVP to an Event. We may collect (through a third party payment processor) payment information, including your name, credit card information, and billing information, in order to make payments to the Chefs for Events you attend or charge you for Orchid's fees (as described in our Terms of Service located here: http://Orchideats.com/termofservice). If you provide us feedback or contact us via e-mail, we will collect your name and e-mail address, as well as any other content included in the e-mail, in order to send you a reply. When you participate in one of our surveys, we may collect additional personal information. When you post messages through the Services and/or comment on content available through the Mobile App and Site, the information contained in your posting and/or comment will be stored on our servers and other users will be able to see it. We also collect other types of personal information that you provide to us voluntarily, such as your operating system and version, product registration number, and other requested information if you contact us via e-mail regarding support for the Services. If you participate in a contest, sweepstakes or other promotion, we will collect any data you provide in connection with that promotion. We may also obtain information from other sources and combine that with information we collect on our Services. We may also collect personal information at other points in our Mobile App and Site that state that personal information is being collected.

(b) Information Collected via Social Networking Sites. The Services may allow users to connect with various social networking sites (each an "SNS"). By connecting with an SNS, you are allowing the Services to access your information and you are agreeing to the SNS Terms of Use in your use of the Services. We may receive information from an SNS and that information may be imported to the Mobile App and Site (either as a part of your Profile or otherwise). Once you connect with an SNS, you may be able to post recent activity or content back to the SNS. Any information that we collect from an SNS may depend on your SNS privacy settings, so please consult the SNS' privacy and data practices. Further, you can edit privacy settings via the SNS, or disable the connection with the SNS by visiting the applicable SNS.

(c) Information Collected via Technology.

Information Collected by Our Servers. To make our Mobile App and Site and Services more useful to you, our servers automatically collect information from you, including your browser type, operating system, Internet Protocol (IP) address, domain name, and/or a date/time stamp for your visit. We use your IP address to diagnose problems with our server, report aggregate information, determine the fastest route for your computer to use in connecting to the Mobile App and Site, and administer and improve the Mobile App and Site. Log Files. We gather certain information automatically and store it in log files, including IP addresses, browser type, Internet service provider (ISP), referring/exit pages, operating system, date/time stamp, and clickstream data. We use this information to improve the content of the Mobile App and Site, market research purposes, to communicate information to visitors and to better tailor our Services to our users' needs. Cookies. Like many online services, we use cookies to collect information. "Cookies" are small pieces of information that a website sends to your computer's hard drive while you are viewing the website. We may use both session Cookies (which expire once you close your web browser) and persistent Cookies (which stay on your computer until you delete them) to provide you with a more personal and interactive experience on our Mobile App and Site. This type of information is collected to make the Mobile App and Site more useful to you and to tailor the experience with us to meet your special interests and needs. How We Respond to Do Not Track Signals. We do not currently respond to "do not track" signals or other mechanisms that might enable consumers to opt out of tracking on our Mobile App and Site. Analytics. We use analytics services (including, but not limited to, Google Analytics) to help analyze how users use the Mobile App and Site. Analytics services use Cookies to collect information such as how often users visit the Mobile App and Site and we use the information to improve our Mobile App and Site and Services. The analytics services' ability to use and share information collected by them is restricted by their Terms of Use and Privacy Policy, which you should refer to for more information about how these entities use this information, and how you can opt-out of their use of such information. Email Delivery Service. We may use email delivery services (including, but not limited to, SendGrid) to help us deliver, analyze and manage emails. The email delivery service may use Cookies, pixels or other technologies to track open rates, clicks and other metrics. We may use these email delivery services for our marketing and promotion purposes.

We reserve the right to use anonymous data for any purpose and disclose anonymous data to third parties in our sole discretion. We may get information about you from other sources. We may add this to information we get from this Mobile App and Site.

How we ensure a safe experience


At Orchid Eats, we take trust and safety very seriously. We strive to create a memorable and positive food experience for all of our users. Your health and well being are paramount to us and we are committed to providing a safe and trusted marketplace for customers. Although chefs on our platform are ultimately responsible for food safety, they are personally vetted by the Orchid Eats team and are held to high standards.

We have created our own interview and vetting process that each chef must complete before they can offer meals on the marketplace. Orchid Eats merely provides a platform for independent chefs to offer their meals and grow their business. Accordingly, food safety remains the ultimate responsibility of the chef from whom you purchase. If you have any questions or concerns, please contact us at param@orchideats.com.

Chef Interviews

As part of our vetting process, all chefs active on the marketplace are required to complete an interview with one of our Chef Support Team members. During the interview, chefs go through a multi-step vetting processes, which includes: a thorough in-home inspection, face-to-face interview, live cooking demonstration, taste test of sample dishes, and verification of identification.

Food Handler Certification

Orchid Eats requires that all chefs active on the marketplace complete their state's Food Handler course for food safety training. Before they are allowed to post their first meal, each chef must provide proof of course completion. All food-service employees in restaurants or catering companies serving food are required to obtain a Food Handler Card.
